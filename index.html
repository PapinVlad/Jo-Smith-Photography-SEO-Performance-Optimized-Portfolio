About Jo Smith Photography

Your Story, Beautifully Captured

Hello! I'm Jo Smith, a passionate wedding and portrait photographer based in beautiful Dundee, Scotland. As a recent photography graduate, I bring fresh creativity and contemporary techniques to every photoshoot. My mission is simple: to capture the authentic emotions and precious moments that tell your unique story.

What sets me apart is my collaborative approach to photography. From helping you choose the perfect outfits and locations to guiding you through natural poses that showcase your personality, I'm with you every step of the way. I believe the best photographs happen when you feel comfortable and confident, which is why I focus on creating a relaxed, enjoyable experience during every session.

Ready to create beautiful memories together? Let's capture the moments that matter most to you!

Professional Portrait Sessions

Whether you need headshots for your business, lifestyle portraits for social media, or artistic portraits that capture your personality, I create images that truly represent who you are. Perfect for professionals, creatives, and anyone wanting beautiful portraits.

Romantic Couple Photography

Celebrate your love story with intimate couple portraits that capture your connection. Perfect for engagements, anniversaries, or simply because you want to document this beautiful chapter of your relationship. Each session is tailored to reflect your unique bond.

Family Portrait Sessions

Capture the joy and love of your growing family with relaxed, natural family portraits. I specialize in making children feel comfortable while creating beautiful images that showcase your family's personality and the special bonds you share.

Maternity Photography

Pregnancy is a magical time that deserves to be beautifully documented. I create elegant, artistic maternity portraits that celebrate this incredible journey. Sessions are typically scheduled between 28-36 weeks and can be held in-studio or at scenic outdoor locations around Dundee.

Pet Photography

Your furry family members deserve professional portraits too! I capture the personality and spirit of your beloved pets, whether it's playful action shots in the park or elegant studio portraits. Perfect for pet lovers who want to celebrate their four-legged companions.

Wedding Photography

Your wedding day is one of life's most precious moments, and I'm honored to document every beautiful detail. From intimate elopements to grand celebrations, I capture the authentic emotions, candid moments, and elegant details that make your day uniquely yours. Let's create timeless memories you'll treasure forever.
